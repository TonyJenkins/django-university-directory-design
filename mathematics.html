Department of Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics at the University of Poppleton offers a vibrant academic environment where pure and applied mathematics flourish. Our faculty members are dedicated to advancing mathematical knowledge while providing exceptional education to our students.

Faculty Members

  • Professor David Anderson

    Professor of Pure Mathematics & Department Head

    Email: d.anderson@poppleton.ac.uk

    Professor Anderson's research interests include algebraic topology, category theory, and homotopy theory. He has published extensively in leading mathematical journals.

  • Dr. Maria Garcia

    Associate Professor of Applied Mathematics

    Email: m.garcia@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Garcia specializes in mathematical modeling of biological systems, differential equations, and computational mathematics.

  • Dr. Thomas Brown

    Senior Lecturer in Statistics

    Email: t.brown@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Brown's expertise encompasses statistical inference, Bayesian methods, and experimental design with applications in medical research.

  • Dr. Jennifer Lee

    Lecturer in Mathematical Physics

    Email: j.lee@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Lee focuses on quantum mechanics, relativity theory, and the mathematical foundations of modern physics.

  • Dr. Michael O'Brien

    Assistant Professor of Number Theory

    Email: m.obrien@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. O'Brien researches algebraic number theory, cryptography, and the distribution of prime numbers.
