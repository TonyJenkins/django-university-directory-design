Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Poppleton is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, advancing knowledge in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software engineering, and more.

Faculty Members

  • Dr. Sarah Mitchell

    Professor of Computer Science & Department Head

    Email: s.mitchell@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Mitchell's research focuses on artificial intelligence and machine learning, with particular emphasis on natural language processing and neural networks.

  • Dr. James Chen

    Associate Professor of Cybersecurity

    Email: j.chen@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Chen specializes in network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. He leads several research projects on protecting critical infrastructure.

  • Dr. Emily Thompson

    Senior Lecturer in Software Engineering

    Email: e.thompson@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Thompson's expertise lies in agile methodologies, DevOps practices, and large-scale software architecture design.

  • Dr. Rajesh Patel

    Lecturer in Data Science

    Email: r.patel@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Patel focuses on big data analytics, data visualization, and statistical modeling for real-world applications in business and healthcare.

  • Dr. Alice Williams

    Assistant Professor of Human-Computer Interaction

    Email: a.williams@poppleton.ac.uk

    Dr. Williams researches user experience design, accessibility, and the development of intuitive interfaces for diverse user populations.
